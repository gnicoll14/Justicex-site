Evidence-quality scoring · AI-assisted · you and your counsel confirm ›

Two objective measures, side by side, for each party — overall and by component. Evidence quality is how well-supported and internally consistent a position's figures are — not a judgment of legal merit or outcome; completeness is how much of the needed evidence is in. They are scored the same way for both sides.

This informs your decisions; it does not make them. Feedback is about evidence completeness and quality — including whether figures reconcile and match your own documents. JusticeX flags objective inconsistencies only; it never judges truthfulness or intent.
