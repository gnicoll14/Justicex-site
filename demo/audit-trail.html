Delgado — Audit Trail

Every action on your matter is recorded as a signed, append-only event and chained together, so the history is complete and cannot be changed without detection. Nothing happens on your matter without an audit event.

How the chain works. Each event carries a fingerprint (a SHA-256 hash) that is computed from the event and the fingerprint of the event before it, forming a chain back to the first event. Each event is also signed (Ed25519). Change any past event and every fingerprint after it stops matching — so tampering is immediately visible. The log is append-only; entries are never edited or deleted.
