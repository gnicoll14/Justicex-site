Overview

JusticeX is…

An objective, fact-based comparison of both sides — built to reach a resolution the easy way. Software that informs every party equally.

What you get

A clear view of where each side stands, what's missing, and where you agree — and a written agreement at the end. Faster, lower cost, less stress.

What to have ready

Your finances, anything about children or custody, and key documents. Add what you have — JusticeX shows what's still needed.

How to start

1 Pick your role.   2 Invite the other party — and a mediator or attorney if you want.   3 Follow the guided steps.   4 Reach a resolution.
