The problem

Disputes are costly and contentious — and your current tools don't resolve them.

The legal hotline
An EAP legal line triages and refers — a short phone consult, then a hand-off to an outside attorney who may not even take the case. It rarely resolves the matter.
The legal plan
A group legal plan is a personal-legal benefit routed to outside attorneys — and it explicitly excludes employment and any dispute where the company is adverse.
The gap
Neither actually resolves the dispute in front of you — so it escalates, drains time, spends legal budget, and strains the relationship and your reputation.

You want disputes to resolve — fairly, at lower cost and effort, with both sides more satisfied and far less contention — across everyone you serve, inside and out.
