One platform. Three ways to resolve.

One neutral engine and one professional directory — whether both sides resolve together, one side prepares a filing, or a contract needs a fair path if a dispute arises. Objective, symmetric, and built around an amicable resolution.

Flagship

Two-Party Resolution

Both sides compared objectively and symmetrically — points of alignment and convergence options, then an agreed memorandum.

  • Divorce, custody & support
  • Commercial & partner disputes
  • Employment, HOA & insurance

Documentation Preparation

One-sided, attorney-in-the-loop preparation of filings and petitions — a starting point for counsel review, never a substitute.

  • Article 81 guardianship
  • Attorney-grievance
  • Petitions & agency filings

Contracts

Assemble agreements with an optional mediation-before-litigation clause that opens a JusticeX matter if a dispute arises.

  • Prenup, cohabitation, separation
  • Employee, partner & vendor terms
  • Individual-editable or org-configured

Objective Comparison & Gap Analysis

Completeness and evidence-quality signals, factual gap maps, and symmetric summaries — never a legal-merit score or legal advice.

Professional Directory & Referral

Route to a neutral mediator, attorney, or financial/tax professional when a matter needs one — no-fee, symmetric, consent-based.
