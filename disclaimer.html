Legal Standard disclaimer

Important legal disclaimer.

JusticeX.ai is a Software-as-a-Service platform that provides objective, symmetric comparison only. It is not a law firm, mediation provider, or arbitration service; it does not provide legal advice or render legal opinions, and the parties reach their own agreed view.

Nature of the platform

JusticeX.ai provides software that facilitates end-to-end dispute-resolution workflows, including case organization, document handling, secure communication, scheduling, and access to artificial-intelligence (AI) features and publicly available legal materials. Where the platform surfaces statutes, regulations, case law, or other legal precedent, it does so without interpretation, recommendation, or opinion as to outcome.

JusticeX.ai does not practice law, provide legal advice, render legal opinions, represent parties before any court, agency, or tribunal, or act as an advocate, counselor, mediator, or arbitrator. No attorney-client, mediator-party, or fiduciary relationship is created by accessing or using the platform.

AI-generated output

AI features available on or through the platform may summarize materials, identify themes, and produce structured, objective feedback on the completeness, internal consistency, or general strength of a submitted position. AI output is generated by automated systems, may contain errors, omissions, or fabricated content ("hallucinations"), and does not constitute legal advice, a legal opinion, or any prediction of how a court, agency, mediator, or arbitrator may rule. All AI-generated content must be independently reviewed and validated by the user and, where appropriate, by a licensed attorney or qualified neutral, before it is relied upon for any purpose.

Responsible-AI & UPL standard

JusticeX.ai builds its unauthorized-practice-of-law and responsible-AI safeguards to be consistent with the guardrail model established for AI in the legal sector, including Anthropic's Claude for Legal: every AI output is treated as a draft for attorney review — not legal advice or a legal conclusion; legal citations carry source attribution, and any reference not verified against an authoritative source is flagged rather than presented as settled; explicit gates sit before anything is filed, sent, or relied upon; the platform applies conservative defaults on privilege and on subjective legal calls; and jurisdiction assumptions are surfaced. JusticeX applies these standards, on a going-forward basis, to every feature and disclosure.

It is further JusticeX.ai's policy to use Claude and any third-party connectors or partner tools in compliance with Anthropic's applicable Usage Policy — including its high-risk-use requirements for legal services, namely human oversight and disclosure of AI use — and with the terms of those third-party providers, now and on a continuing basis. This reflects a shared standard of care and a commitment to comply with the governing policies; it is not a partnership with, sponsorship by, endorsement by, or certification from Anthropic or any AI provider.
